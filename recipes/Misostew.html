How to cook Misostew

Ingredients

  • Beef meat
  • Onion
  • Zucchini
  • Potato x 3
  • Tofu
  • Mushroom
  • Spring onion
  • Chilli pepper
  1. Boil water then put beef meat and onion.
  2. Cut potato into slice then put potatoes
  3. Cut zucchini into chunk then put into the stew
  4. Put mushroom
  5. Put tofu
  6. Add chilli pepper and spring onion on top
